About Me

I am an M.Tech student specializing in Communication and Signal Processing at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Indore. With a strong foundation in ECE and a GATE AIR 1999, my work focuses on the intersection of hardware architecture and high-performance algorithmic development.

My expertise spans from VLSI design (Cadence, Verilog) to implementing complex DSP algorithms and exploring Machine Learning applications in signal analysis. I bridge the gap between theoretical modeling and physical implementation, aiming to create hardware-efficient engineering solutions.

Deeply interested in the "human" side of logic, I find creative balance through poetry and philosophy, which often informs my approach to complex problem-solving.
