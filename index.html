Research Publications

  • Enhancing Two-Area Interconnected Power Systems Using Model Predictive and PID Control Approaches

    IEEE

    This research investigates advanced control strategies for enhancing stability and performance in two-area interconnected power systems. The study compares Model Predictive Control (MPC) with traditional PID control approaches, demonstrating significant improvements in transient response, stability margins, and disturbance rejection capabilities under varying load conditions.

    Key Contributions:

    • Development of optimized control algorithms for frequency regulation in multi-area power systems
    • Comprehensive comparative analysis of MPC and PID control strategies for power system stability
    • Novel simulation framework for testing control methodologies under various disturbance scenarios
